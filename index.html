ABOUT ME

Hello, I'm Nur Athilia Sofia, a 19-year-old with roots nestled in the charming town of Kuantan, Pahang. My journey in education began at Sekolah Kebangsaan Bandar Teknologi Kajang, as I was raised in Semenyih, Selangor. Later, I went to Sekolah Menengah Kebangsaan Engku Husain, where I sharpen not only my academic skills but also my character.

Continuing my educational journey, I am now embarked on a new chapter at Institut Latihan Perindustrian Kuala Langat, where I am pursuing further academic excellence.

As I stride forward in this new environment, I remain committed to upholding the values of leadership and teamwork instilled in me during my time as a high school student. Moreover, I am eager to explore new opportunities. With a steadfast determination and a hunger for experience, I look forward to embracing the adventures and possibilities that lie ahead.
